Pratexo = A Catalyst for Sustainable Change

Why we are here:

We are here to use technology as a force for good. We are on a mission to unlock the full potential of edge and distributed computing. We not only believe, but know, that edge and distributed computing is needed to succeed in transitioning into smarter, greener, cleaner and safer societies. We are here to help our clients scale and accelerate their journey towards reaching their sustainability goals.

What we do:

By utilizing our technology, companies can dramatically improve their operations and lower overall CO2 emissions.

Power Company in Norway

Focus: Improving the efficiency of the electrical grid

By using Pratexo’s edge platform, HKN is working to increase the uptime of the system from 99.5% to 99.999%, allowing even more mission critical systems to be taken off of liquid fuels and out of the carbon cycle.

Learn more here.

European commercial windmill monitoring company

Focus: Reduce impact of windmills on bird populations

Edge technology and artificial intelligence is used to monitor and identify birds near windmills, enabling the windmill to alter its operations in real time. Making windmills safer to bird populations will ensure the expanded use of this critical technology to generate green electricity.

Global cement company

Focus: Reduce CO2 emissions

Internet of Things sensors generate data that is processed by machine learning algorithms running in the plant ‘edge’. By processing this data in real time, machine uptime and efficiency are maximized, helping to reduce CO2 emissions.
